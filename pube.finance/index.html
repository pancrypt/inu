About $PUBE

Community Project

PUBE is a community-led project. No presale, no airdrop, 100% fair launch. The liquidity pool is locked for the protection of the community

10% Smart Fee

5% of every transaction is automatically distributed to holders as passive rewards. The other 5% is added to the liquidity pool used by exchanges

Changing The World

How to Buy

1. Download Trust Wallet

Visit trustwallet.com and download the app. TrustWallet is the most secure cryptowallet (MetaMask with BSC connected is a secure alternative). Make sure to safely store your seed phrase.

2. Add some BNB

Buy some BNB through the app. These purchased BNB coins will be used to purchase PUBE on the Binance Smart Chain.

3. Go to the DApps tab and click on PancakeSwap

PancakeSwap is a decentralised exchange that allows users to swap BNB with BSC coins like PUBE. iPhone users must enable trust browser before doing so.

4. Swap for $PUBE

Click ‘Select A Currency’ and enter the contract:

0x3916984fa787d89B648Ccd8d60B5ff07E0E8e4F4

Remember to put your slippage to 12%
