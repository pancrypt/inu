About $PUBE

Community Project

PUBE is a community-led project. No presale, no airdrop, 100% fair launch. The liquidity pool is locked for the protection of the community

10% Smart Fee

5% of every transaction is automatically distributed to holders as passive rewards. The other 5% is added to the liquidity pool used by exchanges

Changing The World

PUBE uses a Reserve Fund to donate to important causes around the world. Our community has already donatedto several charities

How to Buy

1. DownloadTrust Wallet

Visittrustwallet.comand download the app. TrustWallet is the most secure cryptowallet (MetaMaskwith BSC connected is a secure alternative). Make sure to safely store your seed phrase.

2. Add someBNB

Buy someBNBthrough the app. These purchased BNB coins will be used to purchasePUBEon theBinance Smart Chain.

3. Go to theDAppstab and click onPancakeSwap

PancakeSwapis a decentralised exchange that allows users to swap BNB with BSC coins likePUBE. iPhone users must enable trust browser before doing so.

4. Swap for$PUBE

Click Select A Currency and enter the contract:

0x3916984fa787d89B648Ccd8d60B5ff07E0E8e4F4

Remember to put your slippage to 12%
